How to Play

Here's a list of the current commands as of FYFv1.0:

fight help - See these (or updated) commands.
fight monster - Go Monster hunting.
fight @slackbot - Challenge slackbot to a fight.
forefeit - Concede victory to your opponent.

status - Get your status, or that of your current fight.
status moves - See what moves you can use.
status items - See what items you can use.

use `move` (e.g. use Attack) - Use a move!
equip weapon `item` - Equip item as a weapon.
equip armor `item` - Equip item as armor.

craft - Begin crafting items or moves
craft `response` - Respond to the crafting prompts

fight ping - Remind your opponent to fight.
item `item` - See more about item.
item drop `item` - Drop item.

Enjoy!
